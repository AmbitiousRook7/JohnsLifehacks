Hack Your Life: 5 Clever Tricks for Smarter Living

1. Shine Your Teeth

  1. Add a pinch of salt to your toothpaste before brushing.

  2. Brush your teeth gently in all directions.

  • The salt helps remove yellowish stains and contains chlorine to kill bacteria, leading to whiter teeth.

2. Recover Lost Items

  • If you've lost small items like earrings or keys:
  1. Cover the mouth of a vacuum cleaner's pipe with a piece of cotton cloth.

  2. Secure it tightly so it doesn't get sucked in.

  3. Turn on the vacuum and use it to find and pick up the lost items.

3. Store Natural Peanut Butter

  • Store the peanut butter jar upside down to prevent the oil from separating.

  • This makes it easier to mix when you're ready to use it.

4. Remember Where You Put Things

  • When you put something down temporarily, say it out loud (e.g., "I've put my phone on the dresser").

  • This engages more areas of the brain and creates a richer memory, making it less likely you'll forget where you put it.

5. Decision-Making Coin Flip

Coin flips have always been a good way of making a decision; however, this hack uses coin flips in a way you would not expect.

  • If you're struggling to make a decision, flip a coin.

  • While waiting for the result, your mind will start to wish for what it wants, helping you realize your true preference.
